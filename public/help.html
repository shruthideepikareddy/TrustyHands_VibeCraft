Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel a booking?
You can cancel your booking through the 'My Bookings' section in your profile. Cancellations made 24 hours before the service are eligible for a full refund.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking. Cash on delivery is also available for specific services.
Are the workers verified?
Yes, every professional on Trustyhands undergoes a rigorous background check and identity verification process to ensure the highest standards of safety and quality.
How do I reset my password?
Go to the login page and click on 'Forgot Password'. You will receive an email with instructions to reset your password.
How can I contact customer support?
You can reach our 24/7 support team via the 'Contact Us' page, or email us directly at support@trustyhands.com.
