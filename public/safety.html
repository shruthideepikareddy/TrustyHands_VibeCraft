Your Safety is Our Priority

At TrustyHands, we take your security and well-being seriously. Here are the steps we take to ensure a safe experience for everyone.

Safety Measures

Verified Workers

Every service provider on our platform undergoes a multi-step verification process, including identity checks (Aadhar/ID) and criminal background screening.

Secure Payments

Transactions are processed through secure gateways. We recommend paying through the platform to ensure a clear record and protection of your funds.

Safe Communication

Keep all conversations within our messaging system. This helps us support you if any disputes arise and keeps your private contact details secure.

Ratings & Feedback

Honest reviews from real users help keep our community accountable. Always check ratings before booking and leave your own feedback after service.

At-Home Safety

We recommend being present during the service and keeping valuables secure. If you ever feel uncomfortable, our support team is available 24/7.

Reporting Issues

If you encounter any suspicious behavior or have a concern about a service, report it immediately through the app or by contacting our safety team.
